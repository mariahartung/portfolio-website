About

My Story

Hi. I'm Maria and I'm a UX Designer. My background in social science has equipped me with solid research skills I bring to UX design to help companies identify underserved customer needs.

Through my experience in conducting effective interviews, I can uncover needs, goals, and the “why” behind user's interactions with products to help businesses translate insights into features. As a UX Designer, I work with intention and empathy to solve problems and create simple, thoughtful experiences that serve both business and people.

Previously, I interned at refugee aid, where I worked in collaboration with team members to address the needs of people in uncertain situations. After finishing my studies, I wanted to focus on applying my research skills and creativity to build tangible products that have a positive impact on people's lives. This led me to transition to UX design.

I'm currently available for full-time work in the cologne area or remotely. I look forward to work in an innovative team in the e-commerce, education, or health industry to build products that empower people and simplify their lives.

When I'm not designing, you can find me shooting street photography, reading, or playing guitar.
