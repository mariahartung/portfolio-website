My Story

As a UX Designer with 2 years of experience designing B2B and B2C products, I specialize in uncovering user needs and transforming complex workflows into clear, efficient user experiences with measurable user and business impact.

My work sits at the intersection of user needs, business goals, and product strategy. I enjoy working on complex problems, bringing clarity to ambiguity, and translating user needs into scalable solutions that have real impact, such as reducing time-on-task, improving conversion rates, and boosting engagement.

What people value about working with me is my ability to bring clarity to complexity, communicate ideas clearly, and drive things forward in a structured and pragmatic way. I thrive in small, collaborative teams where I can take ownership, think strategically, and stay hands-on in the design process.

Currently, I’m looking to contribute to innovative teams where I can help simplify complex workflows and use data-driven approaches to create user experiences that make a real difference, preferably in the SaaS, Edtech, or Health space.
