My Story

Hi. I´m Maria. Nice to meet you.

I´m a UX designer with a background in social science. Through my background, I acquired strong analytical and empirical research skills that I can bring to UX design to uncover needs, goals and the why behind users interactions with products.

As a UX designer, I work with intention and empathy for users to solve problems for real people. I love to create simple and meaningful experiences that empower people and make their lives easier.

When I´m not designing, I enjoy shooting street photography, reading or playing guitar.
