My Story

Hi. I'm Maria, an aspiring UX designer with a background in social sciences and two years of experience in end-to-end mobile and web application design. My ambition is to help small and medium-sized companies uncover user needs and translate them into meaningful web or mobile experiences through effective user research and UX conception.

Previously, I worked in collaboration with team members at Refugee Aid to address needs and issues of people in uncertain situations. After some time, I wanted to apply my research skills and creativity to build digital products that could solve people's problems on a larger scale. This led me to transition to UX design.

Currently, I'm available for remote and full-time work inside Germany or the EU. I get excited about opportunities where I can leverage product discovery insights and use my skills to create delightful user experiences that empower people and drive business value in the SaaS or EdTech space.
