About

My Story

Hi. I'm Maria and I'm a UX Designer. My background in social science has equipped me with solid research skills I bring to UX design to uncover needs and translate user insights into web app or mobile app features.

Previously, I interned at refugee aid, where I worked in collaboration with team members to address the needs of people in uncertain situations. After finishing my studies, I wanted to focus on applying my research skills and creativity to build products that have a positive impact on people's lives. This led me to transition to UX design.

Currently, I'm available for remote and full-time work inside or outside of Germany. I'm looking forward to working with an innovative team in the e-commerce, education, or health industry to build digital products that empower people and simplify their lives.

When I'm not designing, you can find me shooting street photography, reading, or playing guitar.
