My Story

Hi, I’m Maria, a UX designer with a background in social sciences and 2 years of experience designing B2C and B2B products. I help small and medium-sized companies uncover user needs and translate them into efficient experiences with measurable user and business impact.

Previously, I worked in collaboration with team members at Refugee Aid to address needs and issues of people in uncertain situations. After some time, I wanted to apply my research skills and creativity to build digital products that could solve people's problems on a larger scale. This led me to transition to UX design.

In my work, I value close collaboration, open communication and transparent decision-making to create engaging experiences that solve real problems for users and the business.

I get excited about opportunities in collaborative, product-led organizations where design has strategic influence and uses data-driven approaches to create real impact, preferably in the SaaS, Edtech or Health space.
